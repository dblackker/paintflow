## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips:
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly (1 max)
- Create curiosity
- Include personalization
- A/B test

**Good examples:**
- "Your painting proposal is ready! 🎨"
- "Quick question about your project"
- "{{first_name}}, still interested?"

**Bad examples:**
- "MONTHLY NEWSLETTER - MARCH EDITION"
- "!!!LIMITED TIME OFFER!!!"
- "Painting services available"

### Timing:
- **Best days:** Tuesday-Thursday
- **Best times:** 10am-2pm local time
- **Avoid:** Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (checked out), weekends

### Personalization:
Always use:
- First name
- Project type
- Property address
- Specific details from their inquiry

### Mobile Optimization:
- 60% of emails opened on mobile
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 lines)
- Large buttons
- Clickable phone numbers

### Compliance:
- Include unsubscribe link
- Physical address in footer
- Clear sender identification
- Honor opt-outs immediately

## Email Metrics to Track

### Key Metrics:

**Open Rate:** Target 40-60%
- Subject line effectiveness
- Sender reputation

**Click Rate:** Target 10-20%
- CTA clarity
- Offer relevance

**Reply Rate:** Target 5-10%
- Personalization
- Question quality

**Conversion Rate:** Target 20-30%
- Lead → Estimate scheduled
- Estimate → Approved

**Unsubscribe Rate:** Keep <0.5%
- If higher, you're emailing too much or irrelevant content

### A/B Testing Ideas:

**Subject lines:**
- With vs without emoji
- Question vs statement
- Personalization placement

**Content:**
- Short vs long emails
- Button vs text link
- With vs without P.S.

**Timing:**
- Morning vs afternoon
- Weekday vs weekend
- Immediate vs delayed follow-up

## Copywriting Tips for Painters

### Voice & Tone:
- **Friendly, not formal:** "Hey" not "Dear Sir/Madam"
- **Confident, not pushy:** "I'd love to work with you" not "You MUST hire us"
- **Helpful, not salesy:** Focus on solving their problem
- **Personal:** Use "I" not "we" when appropriate

### Words to Use:
- Transform, refresh, update
- Professional, experienced, trusted
- Quality, craftsmanship, attention to detail
- Warranty, guarantee, peace of mind

### Words to Avoid:
- Cheap, inexpensive (use "affordable" or "great value")
- Perfect (use "excellent" or "beautiful")
- Overpromising ("lifetime warranty" - be specific)

### Painting-Specific Language:
- "Cutting in" (painting edges)
- "Backrolling" (technique)
- "Prep work" (masking, patching)
- "Finish" (matte, eggshell, satin, semi-gloss)
- "Sheen" (same as finish)
- "Accent wall" (one wall different color)
- "Trim" (baseboards, crown molding, door frames)

## Legal Compliance

### Required in Every Email:
- Physical business address
- Unsubscribe link
- Clear sender identification

### CAN-SPAM Act Compliance:
- Don't use deceptive subject lines
- Identify message as ad (if applicable)
- Include valid physical address
- Honor opt-outs within 10 days
- Monitor what others do on your behalf

### TCPA Compliance (SMS):
- Get express written consent
- Include opt-out instructions
- Honor STOP requests immediately
- Don't text before 8am or after 9pm local time
