# Crewmodo Video Tutorial Series

## Strategy: Self-Service Education Without Live Demos

**Problem:** Live demos don't scale, and low price point ($49-149/mo) can't support high-touch sales.

**Solution:** Comprehensive video library that answers 90% of questions, with Parker AI handling the rest.

**Key principle:** Show enough to build trust and enable self-service, but don't reveal every advanced feature publicly.

## Video Series Structure

### Tier 1: Public Marketing Videos (YouTube, Landing Page)

**Goal:** Convert visitors to trial users  
**Audience:** Prospects researching solutions  
**Style:** High production value, marketing-focused  
**Length:** 2-5 minutes  
**Competitive risk:** Low (shows value, not implementation details)

## Video Production Guidelines

### Equipment Needed:
- **Screen recording:** Loom Pro ($15/mo) or ScreenFlow ($169 one-time)
- **Microphone:** Blue Yeti ($130) or similar
- **Editing:** Descript or Camtasia
- **Total investment:** <$500

### Production Process:

**1. Script (Parker writes)**
- Outline key points
- Keep under 4 minutes
- Include timestamps

**2. Record**
- Clean desktop
- Close notifications
- Use demo account with realistic data
- Record at 1080p

**3. Edit**
- Cut pauses and mistakes
- Add zooms on important clicks
- Add captions (Descript auto-generates)
- Add intro/outro bumper (5 sec)

**4. Publish**
- YouTube (public or unlisted)
- Help center embed
- Transcript for SEO

**5. Promote**
- Email to relevant users
- Social media
- In-app notification

### Time Investment:

**Per video:**
- Script: 30 min (Parker drafts, you review)
- Record: 30 min (1-2 takes)
- Edit: 1 hour
- Publish: 15 min
- **Total: ~2.25 hours per video**

**Batch production:**
- Record 4 videos in one 2-hour session
- Edit over next 2 days
- Publish throughout week

## Competitive Advantage Protection

### What to Show Publicly:

✅ **Show:**
- Core workflows (create estimate, send invoice)
- Mobile app in action
- Speed/ease of use
- Customer testimonials
- Before/after results

✅ **Show with context:**
- Specific features (but not how to replicate)
- UI/UX (can't hide it anyway)
- Pricing (transparency builds trust)

### What to Keep Private:

❌ **Don't show publicly:**
- Advanced settings/configuration
- API documentation (gated)
- Exact algorithms (pricing calculations)
- Roadmap
- Internal tools

❌ **Keep for customers only:**
- Advanced feature tutorials
- Integration setup details
- Custom workflow examples
- Data export formats

### The Balance:

**Public videos answer:** "Does this solve my problem?"
**Private videos answer:** "How do I get maximum value?"

Competitors can copy features, but they can't copy:
- Your understanding of painters
- Community you've built
- Specific workflows you've optimized
- Customer relationships

## No-Demo Sales Process

### Instead of live demos, use:

**1. Interactive demo environment**
- Pre-loaded with realistic data
- Visitors can click around
- Guided tour overlay
- "Try it yourself" CTA

**2. Video library**
- Answers 90% of questions
- Available 24/7
- Scales infinitely

**3. Parker AI chat**
- Answers specific questions
- Books calls only for Enterprise prospects
- Qualifies leads automatically

**4. Self-serve trial**
- 14-day free trial
- In-app onboarding
- Email nurture sequence
- Upgrade prompts at key moments

**When to do live demo:**
- Enterprise prospect ($399/mo plan)
- 10+ users
- Custom requirements
- Otherwise, point to videos
