# Crewmodo Demo Video - 90 Second Script

## Scene 1: Problem (0-10s)
**Visual:** Frustrated contractor juggling paper estimates, phone, calendar
**VO:** "Running a painting business means juggling leads, estimates, scheduling, and getting paid. Most tools weren't built for contractors."

## Scene 2: Intro (10-15s)
**Visual:** Crewmodo logo animation
**VO:** "Meet Crewmodo. The CRM built specifically for painting contractors."

## Scene 3: Estimates (15-35s)
**Visual:** Screen recording - creating estimate
**VO:** "Create professional estimates in 2 minutes. Our Good/Better/Best pricing helps you close 30% more jobs. Customers sign online, no printing needed."

**On-screen actions:**
- Click "New Estimate"
- Add customer: John Smith, 123 Main St
- Set Good: $4,500, Better: $5,800, Best: $7,200
- Click Send → Email slides out

## Scene 4: Scheduling (35-50s)
**Visual:** Calendar with drag-drop
**VO:** "Schedule jobs with drag-and-drop. Syncs with Google Calendar so your crew stays in the loop."

**On-screen actions:**
- Drag "Smith Kitchen" from sidebar to Tuesday
- Google Calendar event appears

## Scene 5: Payments (50-65s)
**Visual:** Stripe payment flow
**VO:** "Get paid faster. Collect 50% deposits online with Stripe. Invoices sync to QuickBooks automatically."

**On-screen actions:**
- Customer opens estimate email
- Clicks "Pay Deposit"
- Stripe checkout appears
- Success confirmation

## Scene 6: Mobile (65-80s)
**Visual:** Phone showing responsive UI
**VO:** "Works perfectly on mobile. Manage your business from the job site."

## Scene 7: CTA (80-90s)
**Visual:** Pricing page
**VO:** "Start your free 14-day trial at crewmodo.com. No credit card required."
